Angles Recipe for the Best Cinammon Rolls

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 3/4th cup war milk
  • 2 1/4th teaspoon yeast
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • 1/4th cup bread
  • cups bread flour

For the filling:

  • 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 table spoon ground Cinammon
  • 1/4 cup butter

Instructions

  1. Mix the milk with the yeast, sugar, eggs.
  2. Melt the butter and add to the mixture.
  3. Add in the flour, and mix until combined in to a dough
  4. Knead the dough for 10 minutes,
  5. Transfer the dough into a large bowl anc cover with the plastic wrap.
  6. Leave it somewhere to rise for 2 hours.
